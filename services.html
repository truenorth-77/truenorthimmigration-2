What We Offer

Comprehensive Immigration Solutions

From student visas to permanent residency, True North Immigration provides expert guidance for every immigration pathway — handled by licensed RCICs who genuinely care about your outcome.

01 — Study Visa

Study Visa

Canada is home to some of the world's top universities and colleges. Our team helps you navigate the student visa process from acceptance letter to landing — smoothly and on time.

  • University & college admissions guidance
  • Study permit application & document prep
  • PGWP (Post-Graduate Work Permit) advice
  • Biometrics & medical appointment support

02 — Work Permit

Work Permit

Whether you have a job offer or are exploring open work permit options, we help skilled workers secure the right permit and build a successful career in Canada.

  • LMIA-supported & employer-specific permits
  • Open work permit applications
  • Intracompany transfers & specialized workers
  • Work permit renewals & extensions

03 — Visitor Visa

Visitor & Tourist Visa

Planning to visit family, attend an event, or explore Canada as a tourist? We handle your Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) application from start to finish with high approval rates.

  • Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) applications
  • Super Visa for parents & grandparents
  • Invitation letter assistance
  • eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization)

04 — Permanent Residency

Permanent Residency

Make Canada your permanent home. Our licensed RCICs guide you through Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs, and family sponsorship to achieve your PR status.

  • Express Entry (FSW, CEC, FSTP)
  • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)
  • Family sponsorship & spousal PR
  • CRS score optimization strategies

Family Sponsorship
